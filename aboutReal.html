What Is Our Website About?


->The project “COV-RES” aims at establishing itself as a pathway of solutions for different problems that currently the whole world is facing due to Covid-19 pandemic. Also, it aims to help people to communicate with different other people for their needs and sources. This webdevelopment project will help to reduce the stress of availing information of emergency services.Furthermore, it will make it easier for people to avail different essentials services. This site will also contain various other important information about coronavirus and the covid-19 pandemic. This website/web-application will not only help the elderly people to avail services easily at doorsteps, but also the younger working generation, to keep a track of daily-pol of vaccinated, infected, recovered, and death during this pandemic. The website also has flexibility to update from time to time as per need and services.

What Is The Necessity Of Our Website?


->Objective of the project “COV-RES” is the site where users will be able to see all the information about this pandemic, its root causes, andimpacts. It will also provide the record of vaccinated, infected, recovered and death toll.Further on signing up, according to their needs user will be able to see the data regarding emergency services, essential services and record based on postal codes precisely. Then at any time users will be able to log in and follow up with the information. At whole, the goal is to make an easy way to surf the web based interface where users can find information according to their needs.
